Compliance & Ethics Home Page Compliance & Ethics


The Acme Corporation is committed to conducting business in compliance with applicable laws and regulations and in accordance with the highest ethical standards. Compliance with our legal and ethical obligations is critical for maintaining Acme’s reputation for integrity, honesty and fairness. Compliance is the responsibility of every employee at Acme.

This website provides employees with the links to resources that explain our legal and ethical responsibilities. On this website employees can find Acme’s Code of Business Conduct and related Code material, information about the Legal Compliance and Ethics Committee, compliance and ethics-related courses available on Acme Learning Network and a repository of policies, guidelines and procedures related to Acme’s Code of Business Conduct.
